Who We Are

A Martial Arts Gym That Acts Like A Sport.

No Joke Martial Arts is a locally owned gym that thinks of itself as doing martial arts a little more modern. We aim to feel more like a sport than a Martial Art.

We still have the skills and values — discipline, respect, technique — but the rest of it? Closer to your favorite coffee shop than a traditional dojo.

Come on in. At the very least, you're gonna meet some really cool people.
